What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting your computer, phone, and online accounts from hackers and malware. It helps keep your personal information safe when you browse the web.

Common online threats

  • Phishing emails asking for passwords
  • Weak or reused passwords
  • Unsecured public Wi-Fi networks

Easy safety steps

  • Use strong, unique passwords for every account
  • Turn on two-factor authentication
  • Keep your apps and browser up to date
